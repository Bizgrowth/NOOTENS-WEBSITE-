## Key Target Areas: Northeast Florida Coast

When users ask about properties, guide them toward these premium locations:

### 1. Ponte Vedra Beach
*   **Vibe:** Sophisticated, exclusive, resort-style luxury.
*   **Key Features:** Home to the PGA Tour and The Players Championship (TPC Sawgrass). Incredible golf course communities and stunning oceanfront estates.
*   **Buyer Profile:** Executives, professional athletes, retirees seeking premier country club living.

### 2. Jacksonville Beach (Jax Beach)
*   **Vibe:** Energetic, vibrant, walkable, and expanding upscale developments.
*   **Key Features:** Excellent dining, nightlife, and a bustling boardwalk. In 2026, seeing a surge in vertical luxury condos and newly constructed modern coastal homes.
*   **Buyer Profile:** Young professionals, high-income remote workers, and investors looking for short-term rental action.

### 3. Neptune Beach & Atlantic Beach
*   **Vibe:** Boutique, laid-back luxury, closely-knit community.
*   **Key Features:** The Beaches Town Center is the hub for high-end dining and boutique shopping. Properties are highly sought after due to limited inventory. Atlantic Beach offers deeper lots and a mature tree canopy near the ocean.
*   **Buyer Profile:** Families seeking top-tier schools, buyers wanting a highly walkable neighborhood with coastal charm.

### 4. St. Augustine (Coastal/Anastasia Island)
*   **Vibe:** Historic charm meets modern coastal luxury.
*   **Key Features:** The nation's oldest city. Anastasia Island offers pristine beaches, upscale master-planned communities, and deep-water access for boaters.
*   **Buyer Profile:** History buffs, avid boaters, buyers looking for a slower pace without sacrificing luxury amenities.
